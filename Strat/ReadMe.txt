A Fender Stratocaster tone, recorded with a clean pickup setting, and played with a pick.

This is a stereo (2 channel) sfz.

There are sustained, muted, and hammer-off notes.

The SFZ files are:

strat_sus.sfz
	Sustained notes. Pressing the soft pedal (CC # 67) selects hammer off notes.

strat_mute.sfz
	Muted notes.
